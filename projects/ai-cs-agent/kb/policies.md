# AgentsFactory Policies

## Refund Policy
- Pro plan subscribers can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase
- Enterprise contracts follow the terms in the custom agreement
- Usage-based overages are non-refundable
- Refund requests must be sent to billing@aidentify.ai
- Processing time: 5-10 business days

## Acceptable Use Policy
Users must not:
- Use agents to send spam or unsolicited communications
- Attempt to reverse-engineer or bypass platform security
- Create agents designed to deceive or defraud
- Use the platform to generate malicious code without authorization
- Share account credentials or allow unauthorized access
- Violate any applicable laws or regulations

## Data Retention Policy
- Conversation logs: retained for 90 days (Pro), 365 days (Enterprise)
- Agent configurations: retained until account deletion
- Backup data: retained for 30 days after deletion
- Upon account closure, all data is permanently deleted within 90 days
- Users can request immediate data deletion via support@aidentify.ai

## Privacy Policy Summary
- We collect: account info, usage analytics, billing data
- We DO NOT: sell your data, use it for advertising, share with third parties
- Agent conversations are encrypted end-to-end
- You retain all rights to your data
- Full policy at https://aidentify.ai/privacy
