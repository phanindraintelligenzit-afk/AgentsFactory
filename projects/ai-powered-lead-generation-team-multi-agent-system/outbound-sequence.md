### CAN-SPAM Compliance Checklist
- ✅ Physical mailing address in every email
- ✅ Unsubscribe link in every email
- ✅ Clear sender identity (LeadGen AI Team)
- ✅ Non-deceptive subject lines
- ✅ Opt-out mechanism honored within 10 business days

### Sending Best Practices
- **Send times:** Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM recipient's local time
- **Spacing:** Respect the Day 1/3/5/7/10 cadence; do not send additional emails between touches
- **A/B test:** Rotate subject lines on Touch 1 (highest impact on open rates)
- **LinkedIn sync:** Send LinkedIn DM 1–2 days after the email touch for multi-channel presence
- **CRM tracking:** Log all touches; if prospect engages at any point, move to a "nurture" track with product content

### Metrics to Monitor
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate (Touch 1) | >45% |
| Reply rate (any touch) | >5% |
| Meeting booked rate | >2% of total sequence starts |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.5% per email |
| LinkedIn DM response rate | >8% |
