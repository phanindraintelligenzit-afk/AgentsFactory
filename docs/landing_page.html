August 2, 2026. This is the deadline for high-risk AI systems to comply with Articles 12 (record-keeping), 13 (transparency), and 14 (human oversight). Penalties for non-compliance are up to €15M or 3% of global annual turnover.

How long does it take to integrate?

Most teams are logging decisions within 5 minutes. Install the SDK, add one line of initialization code, and every agent decision is automatically captured. Your first compliance report is available within 2 hours.

Which AI frameworks do you support?

LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, OpenAI Agents SDK, and custom agents. Our framework-agnostic SDK wraps any Python or JavaScript agent. If your agent can be decorated, we can log it.

All audit logs are encrypted at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II ready and GDPR compliant. Data is stored in EU-based data centers. Enterprise tier offers on-premise deployment for full data control.
