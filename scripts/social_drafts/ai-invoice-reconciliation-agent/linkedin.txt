We just open-sourced AI Invoice Reconciliation Agent.

Autonomous multi-agent system that matches invoices to purchase orders, flags discrepancies, and routes exceptions for approval. Eliminates manual AP workflows.

4 AI agents working together:
• Document Parser — Extracts line items from invoices (PDF, email, EDI)
• PO Matcher — Matches invoices to purchase orders and receipts
• Discrepancy Detector — Flags price mismatches, missing items, duplicates
• Approval Router — Routes exceptions to correct approver based on rules

All built autonomously by the AIdentify agent swarm.
