## ORIGINAL TIPS (2)

A. The companies scaling fastest right now aren't the ones with the best AI — they're the ones who figured out which 3-hour daily task to eliminate first. Speed comes from subtraction, not addition.

B. Most "AI strategy" decks are just automation wishlists with a ChatGPT logo. The real strategy is: what decision are you removing from the loop, not what task are you accelerating.
